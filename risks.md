# Risks

## Safety

- Exposed saw teeth and blade tip are cut/puncture hazards during bowing,
  striking, storage, and transport (design.md "Handle, Grip, And Safety";
  bom.csv edge-guard, storage).
- Uncontrolled blade motion (slip, over-flex, snap-back) can strike the
  player or bystanders.
- Struck-mode tests risk uncontrolled overtones and blade motion (design.md
  "Struck Mode").

## Mechanism / Playability

- Flex/torsion/support state is not repeatable without a jig or reference
  guide; pitch observations may not be comparable session-to-session
  (design.md "Flex Control").
- Bow contact (rosin, angle, pressure, speed) is unresolved; tone may not
  start/sustain/stop cleanly (design.md "Bowed Mode").

## Fabrication

- No blade material, thickness, profile, or steel specification is
  released; any future CAD/DXF must trace to measured coupon data, not this
  concept packet (decision-record.md "Rejected For L1").

## Readiness Boundary

This is an `L1 concept packet`. It is not fabrication authority. Promotion
to L2 requires reviewed parameter names, a measurement plan, a safe
blade-handling strategy, and separation between blade/bowing/flex-control
authority (README.md "Readiness Boundary").
