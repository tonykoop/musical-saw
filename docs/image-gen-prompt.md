# Image-Gen Prompts

Concept image only; not fabrication authority. Do not include cut-ready dimensions, hole locations, or toolpaths.

## Hero Prompt

Photorealistic studio product photograph of a finished musical saw presented as a controlled-flex idiophone rather than a construction tool: a long flexible hand-saw blade of brushed spring steel, tapering from a comfortable wooden handle at one end to a narrow free tip, gently bent into a graceful S curve so the silhouette reads as a playable instrument. The toothed edge is softened and guarded for safe handling, while the smooth back edge is presented as the bowing surface; a violin-style bow and a small cake of rosin rest alongside as the primary excitation tools. Implied materials are tempered spring steel with cool metallic reflections and a warm sealed-wood handle. Clean neutral studio background, realistic human-scale cues from the bow and handle, soft directional studio light raking across the curved blade to reveal its flex, three-quarter product-photography angle, approximately 50mm lens, high detail, photorealistic, no text labels.

## In-Context Prompt

Photorealistic concept image of a musician seated in a quiet rehearsal room, holding the musical saw safely by its wooden handle braced between the knees, flexing the spring-steel blade into a stable S curve while drawing a bow across the smooth guarded back edge. Calm focused posture, controlled and deliberate hand placement, the toothed edge kept away from the body. Natural window light, a music stand and a soft protective case visible in the softly blurred background, shallow depth of field, environmental storytelling that frames the saw as a performed instrument; concept visualization only.

## Detail Prompt

Macro photorealistic close-up of the signature bowing contact region on the curved saw blade: bow hair pressed against the smooth, guarded back edge of the bent spring steel, with the subtle torsion and S-curve flex of the metal catching the light, faint rosin dust on the contact line, and a secure hand grip steadying the blade near the handle. Raking light to bring out brushed-steel texture and the gentle curvature, crisp metallic highlights, shallow depth of field, high detail, no measurements.

## Negative / Avoid

No text/logos/watermarks/fake maker marks, no dimension callouts, no blood or danger theatrics, no exposed hazardous grip on bare teeth, no impossible blade twist or implausible mechanism; anatomically plausible hands if a player is shown.
