# Musical Saw Drawing Brief

No drawings exist or are authorized in this pass. This is an `L1 concept
packet` — decision-record.md explicitly rejects CAD, DXF, and fabrication
drawings until measured blade/safety data exists.

## Required first drawings (future, gated)

- Blade survey sheet: source, tooth condition, wear, and hazard notes
  (blocked on VAL-001).
- Flex-reference guide (optional jig), if adopted, once VAL-002 shows a
  repeatable safe S-curve.
- Edge/tooth guard concept sketch, once a guarding strategy is chosen.

## Authority Notes

- No blade length, thickness, profile, or steel specification is known. Any
  future drawing must trace to measured survey/coupon data (see risks.md,
  cut-list.csv), not this concept packet.
- `wolfram/musical-saw-wolfram-model.wl` models only DIMENSIONLESS control
  ratios (active-length ratio, bend/torsion control, pitch-gain factors) —
  it carries no absolute blade geometry or material constants and must not
  be drawn as real dimensions.
